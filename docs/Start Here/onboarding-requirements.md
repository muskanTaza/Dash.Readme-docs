To begin using Tazapay on production, all accounts must complete the onboarding process. This ensures compliance with regulatory requirements and enables a secure money movement network.

# Required Information

1. Business Details
   1. Registered Business Name
   2. Business Registration / Incorporation certificate
   3. Registered Address and Operational Address
   4. Nature of business (products / services offered)
2. Contact
