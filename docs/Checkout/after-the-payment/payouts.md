Tazapay makes payouts to your <a href="https://docs.tazapay.com/docs/bank-account" target="_blank">bank account</a>. By default, new users signing up for Tazapay will receive their payout in a weekly cycle. The day in which you receive your payout would depend on when you sign up for your Tazapay account. For eg: signing up on a Tuesday would mean you will receive your weekly payout on Tuesdays. However, you write to us at [support@tazapay.com](mailto:support@tazapay.com) to receive payouts on the day of your choosing.

In some cases, you might receive an email asking you to upload documents for the release of your payment. This is Tazapay taking necessary risk mitigation measures to keep the network safe. You can reply to the email with the documents or upload it on the dashboard.
