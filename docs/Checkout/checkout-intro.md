Tazapay Checkout is a <a href="https://docs.tazapay.com/reference/create-checkout" target="_blank">low-code payment integration</a> for your website/marketplace that creates a Tazapay-hosted payment page so that you can quickly collect cross-border payments on mobile and desktop devices. The customers can also pay in their local currencies using a variety of payment methods and you can get settled in the currency of your choice. You can collect from and disburse to 173+ countries with one low-code integration.

> 📘 
> 
> <a href = "https://tazapay.com/payment-methods" target="_blank">Check out Tazapay's list of supported countries and payment methods.</a>
